ECO PAWS

ECO PAWS IS AN NPO ORGANISATION THAT STRIVES TO RESCUE, REHABILITATE AND, RELEASE WILDLIFE NEGATIVELY IMPACTED BY CLIMATE CHANGE.

ECO PAWS HAS SANCTUARIES ACROSS THE GLOBE THAT REHABILITATE ANIMALS HABITATS THAT HAVE BEEN LOST DUE TO CLIMATE CHANGE.
